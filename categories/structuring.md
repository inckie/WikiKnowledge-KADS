Raw data captured during elicitation is highly chaotic, filled with conversational filler, missing context, and mixed concepts. To transform this raw data into high-utility organizational knowledge, it must be programmatically categorized into precise typologies.

This ensures that information is routed to the correct structural framework and remains highly accessible to both developers and automated AI tools.
