High-quality documentation is no longer just for human consumption. It serves as the primary training and context data layer for LLM agents, and internal enterprise search engines.

While traditional Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) relies on flat vector chunking, our methodology emphasizes **Graph-based Integration**. By utilizing WikiKnowledge's native hierarchical structure and explicit `[[wiki-links]]`, AI agents can traverse conceptual dependencies accurately rather than relying on fuzzy semantic proximity.

This category focuses on optimizing componentized documentation to serve as a machine-readable graph for advanced AI integration.
