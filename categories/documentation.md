<!-- human:start -->
To build scalable knowledge bases, organizations must abandon linear, narrative-style documentation. Long, sprawling wikis are difficult to maintain and impossible for AI systems to process effectively. 

Instead, KADS employs Componentized Content Modeling, which splits dense documentation into discrete, single-purpose information blocks, and treats documentation as code.
