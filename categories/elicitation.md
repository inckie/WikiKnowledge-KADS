Knowledge elicitation is not a passive act of note-taking; it is an active engineering process. The primary challenge in knowledge management is the "knowledge acquisition bottleneck," a phenomenon where domain experts possess deep operational capabilities but struggle to verbalize their underlying mental models. 

KADS addresses this bottleneck through systematic, structured inquiry frameworks designed to extract these implicit rules. This category covers the methodologies and protocols for effective elicitation.
